Ballistics

Nimoh® Scientific’s suite of small arms ballistics applications targeting the recreational, sporting, hunting and defence shooting fraternity are truly game-changing.
Following the Nimoh® Scientific philosophy of introducing at least one unique, innovative, feature which isn’t simply a marketing, or functionally immaterial, eye-catching, novelty or gimmick, Nimoh® Ballistics introduces the distinctive Virtual Reticle™ technology.
The Nimoh® Ballistics invented Virtual Reticle™ technology allows the visualisation aspect of all digitised Nimoh® Ballistics software applications to accurately depict any manufacturer’s riflescope reticle with completely flawless precision based on the manufacturer’s own reticle subtensions technical information.
For the modern professional rifleman, sniper, sporting shooter or marksmanship aficionado what this means is truly augmented-reality, smart, small arms ballistics applications, accessible to all through intelligent modifications of pre-existing target acquisition technologies.

Bioinformatics

With rapid advances over the last two decades in genomics technology, and equally rapid growth in computer processing power, designing new software applications which take advantage of both progressions, now, and into the future, is the key challenge presented to the field of in silico molecular biology - bioinformatics.
Continuing along with a key theme of Nimoh® Scientific products to date, the in-production gene toolkit focuses heavily on visualisation. In line with the Nimoh® Scientific policy of refraining from “me-too” approaches to new advanced scientific products, the gene toolkit introduces the Nimoh® Projection™ specialised graphical representation of richly annotated genomic and proteomic sequences.
The Nimoh® Projection™ takes the existing motifs in DNA visualisation, long employed by current bioinformatics software, and with the injection of some simple, but not simplistic, yet sophisticated visual and dynamic ideas, creates more elaborately informative static and interactive maps respectively.
The advanced innovations being introduced to modern bioinformatics via the Nimoh® Scientific gene toolkit are not limited to the Nimoh® Projection™ alone. Under development too are several themes covering advanced recording, change control, and simulation activities.

About Nimoh® Scientific

Nimoh® Scientific is legally a company, loosely an organisation, and mostly a set of esoteric ideas brought into reality through interaction with, and harnessing of, modern scientific technologies. If there is a shorter way of putting it then it is:

"Nimoh® Scientific is a breeding ground for turning innovative ideas into tangible products."

All of the technologies built by Nimoh® Scientific, slowly taking shape and turning into real products, used by real people, world-wide, have been initially brewing as Research and Development projects, some going back almost two decades. The core philosophy behind every tool originating from Nimoh® Scientific, is that each digital instrument incorporates at least one discerning, novel idea not seen anywhere else previously.
At present, the sole permanent researcher, designer, developer, and director, is myself, Kofi Nimoh.

In this capacity I have to-date been the exclusive creator of all the products, manuals, and online presence for Nimoh® Scientific, whilst outsourcing peripheral activities such as language translations, some particularly specialised prototyping and testing, and all artwork.
As such, personal experience – i.e. mine, is the lodestar to all nascent incubating ideas, and currently realised products, more often than not starting out as the proverbial personal “itch”. The background to the ideas which subsequently become products means that the Nimoh® Scientific range of offerings is characteristically varied, with scientific tools currently covering the optics, ballistics, life sciences and automotive spaces.
